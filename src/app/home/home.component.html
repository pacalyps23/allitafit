About Me

My name is Alla Vasylyga and I am an ACE Certified Personal Trainer and Nutrition Specialist with more than 10 years of experience in the field, WBFF Pro Fitness Model, and just a person who loves to help others! My mission is to help you be a BETTER YOU: healthier, stronger, fitter, happier. I want you to feel comfortable in your own body, love yourself more and enjoy every day. Remember, you work out and eat healthy because you love your body not because you don’t like it. Everything starts with YOU, your mindset and positive thinking. I will give you the right tools, teach how to use them and the rest depends on you! Let’s do it! My Vision

I believe that with support and right knowledge you can reach any goals regardless age, gender, genetics, metabolism or experience. That’s why I created a program “Educated Client – Successful Client” and invite you to be part of “Stronger Together” community where goals are:

  1. Education for better results, not random and controversial information from the internet that might hurt your metabolism and diminish your believe in achieving your goals, but from scientific sources (ACE, ACSM, NASM, US FDA etc.).
  2. Help you start a heathy lifestyle routine of training and eating, not temporary changes which bring temporary results.
  3. Create a community of healthy and active individuals with the same goals to communicate, support, and motivate each other.
  4. Earn points and win prizes!

I will be your best friend and teacher. I will take your win as my win, and your results as my results. Let me guide you in your fitness journey and you will discover your best self!
